Space Invaders in PyGame


This is my very first game development project. My team and I worked on recreating the classic game Space Invaders inside Python's PyGame. We used assets and sounds that are found throughout the internet, and we created many of the original functions of the game ourselves. Clicking on the name will take you to the GitHub page, which includes a video demonstrating the game.
